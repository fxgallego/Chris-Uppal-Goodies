Licence

These packages are required by other packages in this collection, but I haven't (yet) formally “published” them.

Depending on what else is currently in my Goodies, the prerequisite packages can be a very mixed bag; some trivial, some less so. In general, the more internal documentation (class comments, package comments) they have then the more likely it is that I'll get around to publishing then later (in some form). Unless the comments say something like “this is a load of crap”…

What I mean by not having published them is that any or all of the following may apply:

  • I haven't got around to documenting them.
  • I have only tested them in so far as they are used by the published packages.
  • I don't think they are finished.
  • I don't think they are done right.
  • I may change or withdraw them without notice.

On the other hand, you are more than welcome to make use of anything you like (under the usual terms). If you do, and you let me know, then I'll probably be so pleased that I'll try to promote them to “published” as soon as possible.
